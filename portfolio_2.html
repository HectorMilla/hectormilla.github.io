Covid Case Study

Exploratory analysis of global Covid data from 2020-2022 to find key metrics and identify trends.

Processing Data

Split data into relevant tables using Excel then cleaned and gathered relevant data for later visualization using Microsoft SQL Server. For a deeper look into SQL queries used, take a look at my github repository

Analysis

There are over 600M total cases of Covid around the world with 6.4M recorded deaths. There is a 1.06& chance of death from Covid

Europe, North America, and Asia are the continents with the highest covid related death counts.
*Asia's numbers could be higher but do to insufficient data from China, numbers are lower than expected.

In our time analysis we see that South Korea and Switszerland have the highest Average Percent of Population infected. We can also notice a significant increase in numbers of infected population around December 2021. This could be due to the increase in circulation of delta and omicron variants
